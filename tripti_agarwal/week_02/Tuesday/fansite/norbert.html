    Intersting facts about him

  • In appearance and behavior, Norbert Wiener was a baroque figure, short, rotund, and myopic, combining these and many qualities in extreme degree.
  • His conversation was a curious mixture of pomposity and wantonness.
  • He was a poor listener. His self-praise was playful, convincing, and never offensive.
  • He spoke many languages but was not easy to understand in any of them.
  • He was a famously bad lecturer.
  • He died during a trip to Sweden and left two daughters.
  • His wife was the former Margaret Engemann.
